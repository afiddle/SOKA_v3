# Question
Hyperemesis gravidarum is more common in patients with **multiple gestation**, **hydatidiform mole**, or history of GERD

---

# Answer
Multiple gestation/mole = ↑ hCG + progesterone → ↑ nausea; ↑ relaxation of LES → ↑ vomiting
