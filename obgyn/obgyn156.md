# Question
- Karyotype of Müllerian Agenesis = **XX::XX/XY** and **↔::↓/↑/↔** testosterone levels    - Karyotype of Androgen Insensitivity Syndrome = **XY::XX/XY** and **↑::↓/↑/↔** testosterone levels

# Answer
