# Question
Systemic endothelial dysfunction due to preeclampsia can lead to what systemic condition?   **Hypertension (due to systemic vasoconstriction aka intrarenal artery vasospasm)**

---

# Answer
* leads to decreased production of vasodilators [PGE2, prostacyclin, VEGF] & increased production of vasoconstrictors [TXA2]  == widespread capillary leakage (proteinuria, edema) and vasospasm (hypertension, end-organ hypoperfusion [eg, renal failure]).   This decrease in perfusion leads to chronic placental ischemia, which triggers increased release of antiangiogenic factors throughout the maternal circulation.     These antiangiogenic factors bind and decrease proangiogenic factors such as vascular endothelial growth factor (VEGF) and placental growth factor.
