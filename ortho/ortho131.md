# Question
The common peroneal nerve splits into the deep peroneal nerve (supplying the **anterior** compartment) and the superficial peroneal nerve (supplying the **lateral** compartment)

---

# Answer
deep-anterior | super-lateral  - deep peroneal is most commonly injured at the fibular head / anterior compartment syndrome     - superficial peroneal is most commonly injured due to repeated ankle sprains / fascial defect
