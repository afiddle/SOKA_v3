# Question
The tumor cells of a Schwannoma stain positive for **S-100::Ca2+-binding protein, a marker for neural crest derivation cells**

---

# Answer
S-100 = marker for cells of neural crest derivation (melanocytes and Schwann cells), as well as Langerhans cells and other dendritics
