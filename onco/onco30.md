# Question
**Adenocarcinoma** is the most common cause of primary lung carcinoma (40%)

---

# Answer
adenocarcinoma (40%) > squamous cell carcinoma (30%) > small (oat) cell carcinoma (15%) > large cell carcinoma (10%) > carcinoid tumor (5%)
