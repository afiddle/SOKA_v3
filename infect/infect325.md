# Answer
Streptococcus pneumoniae is the MCC    Most virulent strains S. aureus + bacteriophage = genes for Panton-Valentine leukocidin (PVL), a cytotoxin that destroys leukocytes == tissue necrosis == necrotizing pneumonia
