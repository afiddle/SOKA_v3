# Question

---

# Answer
Reed-Sternberg cell = large cell w/ multiple nuclear lobes or nuclei, large nucleoli, and abundant cytoplasm = owl's eye appearance = Hodgkin lymphoma    bi-nucleated with prominent nucleoi (owl eyes)
