# Question
A saddle-nose (thick) with thick cheeks is characteristic of **Lepromatous** leprosy

---

# Answer
Leonine facies of Lepromatous Leprosy = weak TH1 == TH2 cytokine profile = IL-4, IL-5, and IL-10; can't contain the disease extent
