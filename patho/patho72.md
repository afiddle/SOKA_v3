# Question
Proto-oncogenes are essential for cell **growth** and **differentiation**   * Bonus: What are the main tumor promoter aka protooncogenes?

---

# Answer
- Mutations of proto-oncogenes form oncogenes that lead to unregulated cellular growth    - categories of proto-oncogenes include: growth factors, growth factor receptors, signal transducers, and cell cycle regulators   **Tend to be dominant mutations
