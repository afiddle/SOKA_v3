# Question
One adverse effect of SGLT2 inhibitors is increased risk of **urinary** tract infection

---

# Answer
~ increased urine glucose concentration == UTIs (eg, pyelonephritis), genital mycotic infections (eg, vaginitis), and necrotizing fasciitis of the perineum (ie, Fournier gangrene)
