# Question
What class of drugs acts by closing K+ channels in pancreatic beta cells, causing increased endogenous insulin secretion independent of blood glucose lvls?    **Sulfonylurea drugs (e.g. tolbutamide, glyburide)**

---

# Answer
C-peptide is a marker of endogenous insulin secretion (since proinsulin is cleaved into insulin and C-peptide);  Sulfonylureas == inc endogenous insulin secretion == inc C-peptide 'n' insulin lvls
