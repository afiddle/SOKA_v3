# Question
**Scalloped** **colloid** is a characteristic histological finding associated with **Graves disease**

---

# Answer
"Graves = hyperthyroidism + exophthalmos + pretibial myxedema  diffusely enlarged thyroid, tall and crowded follicular epithelium with hyperactive reabsorption    ""scallops"" are the white spaces adjacent to epithelium  normal x Graves"
