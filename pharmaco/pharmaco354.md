# Question
Cells generate purines for cell growth through either **de novo** production or through the **salvage** pathway

---

# Answer
Cells undergoing large/rapid amounts of proliferation / cell growth require de novo purine synthesis    AMP and GMP will be important to make nucleotides (for DNA/RNA), but also function in various other growth related capacities
