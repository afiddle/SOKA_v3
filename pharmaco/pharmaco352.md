# Question
**Low**-dose Aspirin and **Diuretics** decrease uric acid **secretion**

---

# Answer
- Low dose Aspirin inhibits the OAT secretion channels that carry uric acid from the blood into the renal proximal tubule cell; thus at standard low dosages Aspirin is NOT used for pain control in gout   - Diuretics decrease GFR (less uric acid filtered through the glomerulus)
