# Question
"The constant cycles of demyelination and remyelination seen in Charcot-marie-tooth result in unique histologic findings, including muscle fiber type ""**grouping**""  * Bonus: CMT symptoms?"

---

# Answer
"muscles change from a ""checkerboard distribution"" of type 1 and type 2 fibers to all fibers associated with that nerve being of same type; this is seen in other diseases that cause denervation atrophy"
