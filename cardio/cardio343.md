# Question
Isolated systolic HTN can occur in **aging::most common cause**, severe **AoR aortic regurgitation**, or systemic causes (anemia, hyperthyroidism)

---

# Answer
aging == increased arterial stiffness (~ decreased elastin, increased collagen deposition); ISH = 60-80% of HTN >60yo
