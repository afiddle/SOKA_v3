# Question
Harsh, holosystolic murmur and palpable thrill at the left LSB: **VSD ventricular septal defect**

---

# Answer
Same holosystolic sound as mitral regurgitation, distinguish with location (VSD = left sternal border, mitral at mitral area) and MR is soft, no thrill
