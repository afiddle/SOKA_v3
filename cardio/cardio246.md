# Question
What effect may NSAIDs have on GFR?    **Decreased GFR**

---

# Answer
Prostaglandins dilate the afferent arteriole, and NSAIDs inhibit prostaglandin synthesis == prerenal azotemia (BUN ratio >20:1) ~ afferent arteriole dilatation
