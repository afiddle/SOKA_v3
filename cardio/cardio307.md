# Question
What is the treatment of choice for agitation in an NSTEMI patient with chest pain, agitation, dilated pupils, and normal cardiac enzymes?    **IV benzodiazepines**

---

# Answer
this patient likely is experiencing cocaine toxicity; IV benzodiazepines improve symptoms of agitation, reduce myocardial O2 demand, and alleviate cardiovascular symptoms; aspirin, nitroglycerin, and CCBs are also effective by reducing thrombosis and causing vasodilation respectively (Î²-blockers are contraindicated)
