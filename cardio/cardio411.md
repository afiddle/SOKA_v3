# Question
Acute decompensated heart failure and hypoxemia / respiratory distress should be given **noninvasive ventilation::what form of treatment?**

---

# Answer
respiratory distress due to pulmonary edema; giving oxygen reduces need for mechanical intubation (recall LMNOP for acute decompensated HF)
