# Question
Fast diagnosis: children who reside in homes built before 1978 presenting with **anemia** and **language regression**  **Lead toxicity / poisoning**

---

# Answer
Lead directly inhibits ferrochelatase and Î´-aminolevulinic acid (ALA) dehydratase, resulting in anemia, ALA accumulation, and elevated zinc protoporphyrin levels.
